Summer Housing

Memorable summers with
memorable brothers

Theta Delta Chi is located on MIT's campus, at 372 Memorial Drive alongside the Charles River. TDC is a great place to live for a multitude of reasons:

  • Location - TDC is the closest fraternity to MIT campus, so you can sleep in a little more and get to lab or the 1 bus faster.
  • House culture - Our brotherhood is quite eclectic and unique. Whether you like to jam with others, paint murals, watch movies, or talk about physics and philosophy, you'll find someone to hang out with.
  • Safety - MIT campus is monitored by MIT Police 24/7, so you can ensure peace of mind with us.
  • Roofdecks - We have three roofdecks and a balcony facing the river that allow for beautiful breezes and summertime relaxation.

Pricing

Our housing prices make living at Theta Delta Chi one of the most affordable living situations possible. Rates are for the entire summer.

  • Singles: $1900
  • Doubles: $1700
  • Triples: $1500
  • Quads: $1400
